# FULL STOPS (.)
The full stop is the simplest of the punctuation marks to use.
Generally, full stops are placed at the end of a sentence to signify a break. However, full stops have several other applications in technical writing. 
Rule 1
It is used at the end of a sentence.

Example: She is a happy girl.

Rule 2
It is used at the end of an indirect question.

Example: I am asking what your name is.

Rule 3
It is used in abbreviations.

Example: My month of graduation is Feb. 2016.

Rule 4
It is used at the end of a command.

Example: Stand up.

Rule 5
It used in numbers.

Example: 55.7 (fifty-five point seven)
