# CONTACT DETAILS

## Writing Phone Numbers
Rule 1:
When writing to a local recipient, local phone numbers are to be written as follows: 08021000200

Rule 2:
When writing to a foreign recipient, local phones numbers are to be written as follows: 
(+234) 8021000200

Rule 3:
Foreign phone numbers are to be written as follows: (+123) 80210002000

## Writing Email Addresses
Rule 1:
Write email addresses in lower case. 

Rule 2:
When an email address ends in a sentence, do not follow it with a full stop as it could obstruct users copying the address.

Example:
For more information, you can contact info.ccl@ccl.ng
