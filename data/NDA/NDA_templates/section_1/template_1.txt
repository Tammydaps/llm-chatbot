Purpose of NDA
The purpose of the NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) between CCL and NUIMS on Gas Digitalisation 
is to establish a confidential relationship regarding information related to gas digitalisation, 
encompassing sourcing, market aspects, and infrastructure. This involves developing software or 
a digital platform/technology that helps to integrate gas components taking into consideration source,
infrastructure, and market. This agreement ensures that both parties involved will maintain the confidentiality 
of sensitive data, proprietary technology, strategies, and any other confidential information exchanged during 
discussions or collaborations. Its goal is to protect the shared information from unauthorized disclosure or use, 
fostering a secure environment for collaboration and innovation in the gas digitalisation domain.
